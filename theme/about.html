Welcome to

RPM Arts and Science College

Founded in 2014, KICMA College of Arts and Science is a distinguished institution located in the state of Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. A Self-Financing College, affiliated to the University of Kerala. The institute offers a range of Degree courses, which include 5 UG. These courses are designed to be delivered in Full Time mode. The institute offers various courses such as B.A., B.Com, B.Sc., BBA covering major streams such as Humanities & Social Sciences, Accounting & Commerce, Science, Business & Management Studies. Courses are taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in English, Computer Science and various other fields. The institute have reasonable no of seats and it provides quality education at an affordable fee, making these courses accessible to all, and allowing students to acquire knowledge and expertise in their chosen fields. Moreover, the institute provides excellent infrastructure facilities to support students' learning experiences. In 2020 the name of college was changed to RPM (R.Parameswaran Pillai Memorial) Art's and Science college. State Co-operative Union,kerala is the management of this college. Shri. KOLIAKODE N. KRISHNAN NAIR is the chairman of our college The Institution is situated in eco-friendly campus at Neyyardam covering 12 Acres of green lush environment.The campus is about 29 km from Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Station & KSRTC Terminal and 2.5 km from Neyyardam.
